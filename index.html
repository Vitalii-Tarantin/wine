What is wine-investing
with NFT?

The French wine-investing industry tends to be private and based on long-standing relations between the chateau and merchants. Before there was no possibility to invest in the premium Grand Cru Classe wine segment using a bottle measure.

Winessy aims to allow wine admirers, vintage wine collectors from all over the world to start investing from 1 bottle and get their opportunity to enjoy earning on it. Only 35% of French wine is left to consume outside France. Due to the limits of the allowable production of a particular wine, the exclusivity of vintage wine may keep on growing up to 15-20% per year while the quantity of bottles decreases.

Why try investing
with Winessy?

Digitizing wine bottles while they are still in the chateau barrel turns out to be a win-win technology. Wine is a reliable and stable object for investment that gains its value by being limited, aged and premium level.
